my confession

I know you might be hurting right now. I know I hurt you. And I am so deeply, genuinely sorry.

What I said — comparing you to my ex — was wrong. Cruel. Unfair. There is no excuse for it. She is not better than you. No one is. I said it in anger, and I will regret those words for as long as I live.

Yes, you forgot my birthday. You forgot the month. You forgot the day we first met. And it hurt. It really did. But pain is not permission to say things that cut someone down to the bone. I failed you there.

I know you're on a hard day physically right now, and on top of that, all of this. I wish I had been your comfort instead of your conflict today. That's on me.

You are irreplaceable to me. Not despite your flaws — but wholly, completely, as you are. Every version of you. And I should have shown you that today instead of tearing you down.

Look at us in these photos. Look at how happy we are. That is what is real. That is what I want to protect.

I love you. I'm sorry. Please forgive me.

— always yours
